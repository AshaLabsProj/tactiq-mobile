# Official Release References

The mobile handoff guide should use these current first-party sources:

1. Expo, “EAS Build”: https://docs.expo.dev/build/introduction/
2. Expo, “EAS Submit”: https://docs.expo.dev/submit/introduction/
3. Expo, “Submit to the Apple App Store”: https://docs.expo.dev/submit/ios/
4. Expo, “Submit to the Google Play Store”: https://docs.expo.dev/submit/android/
5. Apple Developer, “Submitting to the App Store”: https://developer.apple.com/app-store/submitting/
6. Apple Developer, “Submit an app”: https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/manage-submissions-to-app-review/submit-an-app/
7. Google Play Console Help, “Prepare and roll out a release”: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9859348?hl=en

Key verified points: EAS Build creates iOS and Android binaries and can manage distribution credentials. EAS Submit can upload those binaries to App Store Connect and Google Play. Store metadata, compliance details, screenshots, testing, and review submission still require completion in the respective store consoles. Google Play submission through EAS requires an appropriately configured Google service account.
